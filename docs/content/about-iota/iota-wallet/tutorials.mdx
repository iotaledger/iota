# Tutorials

## Viewing IOTA Wallet Details
To access details about your IOTA Wallet, such as the wallet version, current network, and installed version, click the settings icon (gear symbol) located at the top-right corner of the IOTA Wallet.

## Viewing Your Wallet Balance
To see your wallet balance and the list of coins and tokens it holds:

1. Navigate to the "Home" tab.
2. Your IOTA balance is displayed prominently, along with any other coins you possess listed below.

## Sending Coins
To send coins to another address:

1. Go to the "Home" tab and select "Send" directly below your IOTA Balance.
2. Select the Coin you want to send.
3. Enter the amount of the coin you wish to send.
4. Input the recipient's address and confirm by clicking "Review."

## Viewing Recent Transaction Details
Your recent transactions, including sends and receives, are listed under the "Activity" tab. Click on a transaction to see more details.

## Viewing and Managing Your NFTs
To view and manage your NFTs:

1. Select the "Assets" tab to see all NFTs you've minted, purchased, or received.
2. Click on an NFT to view more details or to send it to another address.

## Sending an NFT
To send an NFT to another address:

1. In the "Assets" tab, select the NFT you wish to send.
2. Click "Send Asset," enter the recipient's address, and confirm by clicking "Send Asset."

## Locking Your IOTA Wallet
For added security, you can lock your wallet:

1. Click the settings icon (gear symbol) located at the top-right corner of the IOTA Wallet.
2. Select "Auto-Lock Accounts" and set an auto-lock timer by specifying the idle time in minutes, and click "Save."

## Resetting Your IOTA Wallet Password
If you forget your password, it can be reset with your mnemonic recovery phrase:

1. On the login screen, select "Forgot password?"
2. Enter your 24-word mnemonic recovery phrase and click "Continue."
3. Set a new password, confirm it, and select "Reset."


### Viewing Connected Apps

To keep track of which apps you've connected your IOTA Wallet to:

#### Access Active Connections:
1. Within the "Apps" tab, switch to the "Active Connections" view to see all apps currently connected to your wallet.

#### Visit App Sites:
1. To open the website associated with a connected app, click on the app's listing followed by "View."

### Disconnecting from an App

You can easily disconnect your wallet from a connected app.

#### Open Active Connections:
1. Go to the "Apps" tab and select "Active Connections."

#### Disconnect from App:
1. Find the app you wish to disconnect from and click on it, then select "Disconnect."
2. Your wallet will immediately sever the connection with the app, and you'll be redirected back to the Apps tab.
